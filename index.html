How it works

The key to navigating this site is the search bar to the right. This is your control center where all you have to do is type in what you are looking for.


For example try typing in resume or a programming language you are looking for in the box. This will clear out the non related folders and files in the directories section. Using spaces will allow you to look for multiple files at once.

That's all you need to get started.
